Role Assignment Cheat Sheet

Moderation

Founder/Owner (Founder) (Owner)- a role that is the creator/owner of the project

Manager (Manager) - handles crucial things like managment of the project billing, staff, gives instructions, ideas etc

Media Manager (Media Manager) - a role that is responsible for content creators/partners

Head Of Staff (Head Of Staff) - manages staff gives instructions demotes/promotes staff

Senior Administrator (Sr Admin) - A person a role that can give roles below assignments/tasks

Administrator (Admin) - a role that is responsible for Administration ex Chat, Project

Trial Administrator (Trial Admin) - a role in their Administrator's trial period has restricted permissions and is monitored for mistakes

Senior Moderator (Sr Mod) - a role that is responsible for moderation and roles below it like assigning Moderators tasks/assignments to do

Moderator (Mod) - a role that is responsible for modeation of the project for example staff/member moderation

Trial Moderator (Trial Mod) - a role their Moderator's trial period has restricted permissions and is monitored for mistakes

Staff (Staff) - a role that is given to all roles with permissions above it is to be called Staff Team

Design

Lead Designer (Lead Designer) - a role that is responsible for designers example giving them tasks and designs

Designer (Designer) - a role that is responsible for making designs

Designer Team (Designer Team) - a role that is given to designers to refer to them all at once

Development

Lead Developer (Lead Dev) - a role that is responsible for programming/developing the project adding features giving tasks to developers and managing them

Developer (Dev) - a role that is responsible for programming/developing the project adding features etc

Development Team (Dev Team) - a role that is given to developers to refer to them all at once

Media

Partner (Partner) - a role that is given to people who partner with the project

Content Creator/Media (Content Creator) (Media) - given to people who create content base on the project and meet specific requirements for example subscriber/follower count

Support

Support Team (Support Team) - a role that is given to refer to all people on support

Support (Support) - a role that helps members/users they answer members/users questions about the product/service

Demotion

Retired (Retired) - given to people who willingly left the staff/development/design team

Demoted (Demoted) given to people who forcefully got demoted from staff/development/design team
